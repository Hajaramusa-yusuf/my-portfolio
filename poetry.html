Poerty by Feelings

Being messily creative
I would say is the best thing
that can ever happen to me.
Making myself feel
the feelings that are not mine
and describing them
with the blessing of the power of words.
I am alive when I scribble my thoughts.
How are you able to
write about what
you never felt?
they may ask.
Maybe it is a blessing of
being an INFP
to go deep into the imagination of the piece I want to create.
To have the power to feel
what is not my own and
what is my own.
To write what is relatable
to myself and others.
To write a piece that might have a thousand meanings.
Writing is healing
and in it I find solitude
and with it, I know I shall heal.

Nature Heals

Until you try to listen,
nature would be just nature.
The moon would just be
a bright source of light at night.
And the stars would be
just sparkles
in the dark sky.
Until you try to listen,
the ocean would be
just water.
And the desert would be
just a dry land.
Maybe the day you try to listen,
you would find the soul in the moon
and the ray of hope in the stars.
The day you truly listen,
you would hear
the voice of the ocean,
the cry of the forest,
and then maybe,
just maybe,
you would hear the sound of your own tears.

Brown Eye

Your brown eye shines
like the reflection of
the sun on a glass,
full with warmth and passion
I can't contain,
with an irresistible aura
that beams
from them.
Your brown eye, like
the beautiful sunset
it sparkles.

Hope of a Dreamer

Every time I sleep
I see a dream
which in it
my dreams came true.

It like it comforts me
By whispering gently
Into my soul
It is okay to be a dreamer
One day your dreams will paint the sky
like the sun does when it sets.
